Introduction

Welcome to my personal page, created for the UofW Coding Bootcamp Pre-coursework.

I began working in web design in 1993 with a company called Epoch Networks. During that time, I designed the Mirage Hotel's first website, Rodney Dangerfields first website, Gargoyles and Hobie Cat Sunglasses site, among other lesser companies and brands.

After leaving Epoch Netowrks in 1995, I went to work for Microsoft where I worked on the dev team which created some of Microsofts first HTML-based technologies, which would later become products like SharePoint and Commerce Server. We also dabbled in some early concepts of cloud-based services

After leaving Microsoft in 1999, I'd spend the 20 years working on various dev teams as a project manager, technical editor, site producer, and eventually found myself where I am today as a support engineer managing the ServiceNow ITSM Platform for IT Service Mangement.

Where I'm From

I'm a 5th generation Washingtonian! My family came here from England and Scotland in the late 1800s to work in the logging industry. I've lived in Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland, Whidbey Island, Seattle and Renton.
