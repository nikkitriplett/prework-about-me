Introduction

Welcome to my personal page, created for the UofW Coding Bootcamp Pre-coursework.

I began working in web design in 1993 with a company called Epoch Networks. During that time, I designed the Mirage Hotel's first website, Rodney Dangerfields first website, Gargoyles and Hobie Cat Sunglasses site, among other less well known companies and brands.

After leaving Epoch Netowrks in 1995, I went to work for Microsoft where I worked on the dev team which created some of Microsoft's first HTML-based technologies, which would later become products like SharePoint and Commerce Server. We also dabbled in some early concepts of cloud-based services

After leaving Microsoft in 1999, I've spent the last 20 years working on various dev teams as a project manager, technical editor, site producer, and even taught CE classes in HTML and CSS at Highline Community College, but eventually found myself where I am today as a support engineer managing the ServiceNow ITSM Platform for IT Service Mangement.

Where I'm From
